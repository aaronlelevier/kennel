Where to Start

Our Mission

To help make your pets' information more readily available to the people that watch your pets, so that they have it when they need it.


How does it work

Our site has two types of accounts. A Pet Owner account and a Kennel Manager account.


Pet Owner Account

Create a Pet Owner account to add your pets and their information for feeding instructions, care, emergency contact, and more. Then search for Kennel Managers from within your account page or using the search bar at the top of the site. You will be searching for them by Kennel Name, so this works best if the people who watch your pets sign up for an account, and then let you know the Kennel Name that they registered on our site, so you can easily add them. You can add and remove Kennel Managers to you profile based on who watches your pets.


Kennel Manager Account

Create an account so Pet Owners can search for you by your Kennel Name and give you viewing permission of thier pets. Add your information as well that your Pet Owners can view. You can add a profile description, contact information, or other pertinent information. Once a Pet Owner adds you to their list of Kennel Managers, you have viewing permission of their pets' information, and can refer to this any time to help take better care of their pets, or even prepare before their pets arrive.


Other Site Features

The site has a monthly Newsletter, which you can subscribe to on the Home page without signing up for an account, or you can sign up under your account preferences. We will email about one time monthly to alert you on any new feature of the site or relevenat news.

There are some news stories about pets which we will add from time to time that go along with our love for animals and how we like to hear about the amazing things that they do. These can also be found on our Home page.


Thank you, and we hope that you enjoy our site.
